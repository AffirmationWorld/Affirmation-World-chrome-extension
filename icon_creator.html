Icon Creator for Affirmation World

Instructions to Create Icon Files

Step 1: Create the Icon Files

You need to create the following icon files from your logo:

  • icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
  • icon48.png (48x48 pixels)
  • icon128.png (128x128 pixels)

You can use any image editing software like Photoshop, GIMP, or an online tool like iLoveIMG to resize your logo.

Step 2: Save the Icons

Save the resized icons in the "images" folder of your Chrome extension with the following names:

  • icon16.png
  • icon48.png
  • icon128.png

Step 3: Load Your Extension

After saving the icon files, you can load your extension in Chrome by going to:

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  2. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in the top right)
  3. Click "Load unpacked" and select your extension folder

Why Icons Are Important

Icons are essential for your Chrome extension because:

  • They represent your extension in the Chrome toolbar
  • They appear in the Chrome Web Store
  • They help users identify your extension
  • They are required by the Chrome extension manifest
